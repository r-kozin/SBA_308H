Save the Universe!

Earth has been attacked by a horde of aliens!


You are the captain of the USS Assembly, on a mission to destroy every last alien ship.

  • Battle the aliens as you try to destroy them with your lasers.
  • There are six alien ships. The aliens' weakness is that they are too logical and attack one at a time: they will wait to see the outcome of a battle before deploying another alien ship.
  • Your strength is that you have the initiative and get to attack first. However, you do not have targeting lasers and can only attack the aliens in order.
  • After you have destroyed a ship, you have the option to make a hasty retreat.

Additional Options:

Explanation of additional options:

Random Number of Alien Ships(6-10): Enabling this option will randomize the number of aliens that attack. Rather than being 6 alien ships, it will be a random number between 6 and 10 alien ships that attack.

Super Targeting Computer (laser targeting): Scientists have developed a super targeting computer for your lasers. Enabling this option will make it so you choose which of the alien ships you would like to hit with your lasers, rather than only attacking the next alien in the line.

Enabling Shields: Scientists have improved your ship's shields. They don't work that consistently, and only improve your hit points by a random number each time. Each time you are attacked you will randomly generate a shield between 1-5 HP. If the shield is destroyed, any additional damage that was not taken by the shield will be applied to your HP.
